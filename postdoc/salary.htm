Postdoctoral Salary Guidelines

Starting salaries/stipends for all postdoctoral scholars, regardless of their source of funding, are expected to meet the minimum standards set annually by the Office of Postdoctoral Services. A department/school may establish a higher minimum salary/stipend level for postdoctoral scholars paid within that school. Each department/school should establish compensation levels based on experience and the usual standards for payment of postdoctoral scholars/trainees in the relevant discipline.

Effective July 1, 2014 the minimum salary for Postdoctoral Scholars at Virginia Commonwealth University will be $39,000 unless stipulated below:

  • School of Medicine: all new postdoctoral hires must meet the minimum Ruth L. Kirschstein NRSA stipend level. Beginning July 1, 2014, the NRSA minimum stipend level is $42,000. Information on the NIH NRSA Trainee 2014 stipend levels can be accessed here.
  • Department of Psychology; all postdoctoral scholars must be paid at least the NRSA minimum of $42,000.
