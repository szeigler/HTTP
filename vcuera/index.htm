System Status: All systems are currently available.

Beginning August 18, 2010 access to all VCUeRA systems (InfoEd, Click Commerce, and CORES) from any off campus location or through the VCU Wireless system will require you to use VCU's VPN (Virtual Private Network) software provided by Technology Services. Click here for VCU's VPN. If you have any questions or problems accessing the VPN site, contact the Help Desk at (804) 828-2227, e-mail help@vcu.edu, or visit www.ts.vcu.edu/helpdesk.
