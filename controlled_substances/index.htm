Training

All individuals involved in the use of controlled substances must complete training prior to handling any controlled substance. Individuals can self-enroll on the Blackboard Training site, located here.

  1. Log into blackboard using your VCU eID and Password
  2. Click on Courses & Organizations link on the top of the page
  3. Under Course Search, type controlled substances and click Go
  4. Place cursor over appropriate course (for “Authorized Users” or “Registrant”) then click on the down arrow and select Enroll.
  5. After completion of the training module and quiz print a copy of your score.
  6. Authorized users should attach a copy of the score sheet to a completed Personnel Screening Form and give a copy to the Registrant to keep on file.
