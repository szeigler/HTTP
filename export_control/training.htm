Training

The University requires that all individuals with access to export-controlled materials complete the training available through the Collaborative Institutional Training Initiative (CITI).

Instructions: If you have completed training for VCU via CITI in the past, log in using your existing account. If this is your first time, create an account and password by clicking the “New Users Register Here” link on the CITI homepage. Please use your VCU eID username as your username. The password should be different. Following completion of the training, please send a copy of your certificate to exportctrl@vcu.edu.
