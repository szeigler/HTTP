Export Control Laws and Trade Sanctions

Export laws govern both hardware and information in a way that may have an impact on VCU. A number of federal regulations prohibit the export of specific technologies, or participating in foreign boycotts, for reasons of national security, trade protection, foreign policy or based on international agreements. Here we will outline the basics of export control by examining the relationship between export control regulations and specific activities conducted at the university and will explain how the university will comply with these regulations.

VCU is committed to supporting the research activity of our faculty, staff, and students. The information presented here is meant to serve as a resource to ensure compliance with federal laws and regulations regarding export controls while allowing for continued support and collaboration in research.
