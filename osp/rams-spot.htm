RAMS-SPOT Updates & Training

Access RAMS-SPOT

(Research Administration Management System – Sponsored Programs Online Tracking)

The Office of Sponsored Programs (OSP) is converting to RAMS-SPOT, a database and electronic submission and review system. RAMS-SPOT will allow for paperless routing of all major project transactions, paperless record storage, in-system budgeting, in-system communications and will reduce the need for forms. RAMS-SPOT will allow investigators and administrators to readily monitor project review status. RAMS-SPOT will be implemented in phases, beginning in the spring of 2015.
