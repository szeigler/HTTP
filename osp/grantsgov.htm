Grants.gov Information

VCU is registered in Grants.gov as Virginia Commonwealth University. Individual PIs and other users do not need to register.

VCU Information:

VCU is using FileDrop for submission of application packages from the PI to OSP.

FileDrop instructions are available here.

Please upload the Adobe package using FileDrop and forward the packet of IAF, COI and other required forms to dirospa@vcu.edu. For questions, email Blue Team, ospblue@vcu.edu, Green Team, ospgreen@vcu.edu, or Gold Team, ospgold@vcu.edu.
