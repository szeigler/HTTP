We Expect You to Be Your Very Best
VCU’s Third Annual Student Research Week celebrated the research and scholarship of undergraduate and graduate students during a series of events from April 19 to April 27. VCU's First Student Research Week a Success
VCU's inaugural student Research Week was an opportunity for students to learn the research process, receive valuable advice from professors and present their findings to their peers and the VCU community. Research Week was sponsored by the VCU Undergraduate Research Opportunities Program, VCU Graduate Student Association, the VCU Office of Research and the VCU Office of the Provost.
