VCU Innovation Advisory Council

The VCU Innovation Advisory Council, established in May of 2012, consists of industry experts, angel investors, and local entrepreneurs, who provide VCU Innovation Gateway valuable feedback and recommendations regarding critical patenting, commercialization and investment decisions. The panel reviews technologies developed at VCU at quarterly meetings on the basis of patentability, market need, industry feedback, stage of development, and other factors.
