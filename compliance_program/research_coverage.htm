VCU Clinical Research Coverage Analysis

The VCU Coverage Analysis process supports clinical research teams

  • Determining if a clinical study qualifies for coverage of ‘routine care’ costs in accordance with the requirements of Medicare and the U.S. Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act;
  • Documenting a plan for managing costs of clinical services/items by cost type and by appropriate responsible payer; and
  • Complying with essential reporting requirements which support billing compliance over the course of the clinical study.
